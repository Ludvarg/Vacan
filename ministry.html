Vacans Ministry

Information about the ministries in the Vacan sultanate

🕊️ Ministry of External Affairs 🕊️

Emir of External Affairs - Blue Eagle
Manages all external and diplomatic affairs of the nation.
Very restrictive in who can join
Gather info on other nations and world events
Do general diplomacy, negotiations and treaties with other nations
Promote Vacans influence and maintain good ties with the world

🛡️ Ministry of Defense 🛡️

Emir of Defense - Pred20357
Manages the nation’s military stockpile, logistics and planning, not the army itself.
Direct grinding for the military stockpile
Developing plans and doctrines for the army
Keep track of other nation’s military ability and actions

📃Ministry of Internal Affairs 📃

Emir of Internal Affairs - Amon Al Bahr
Ensures the stability, organization and cooperation of the nation.
Manage buerocracy (population, activity, censuses, etc./any statistics needed)
Create maps, statistics or media
Keep tab on what is happening within the nation
Inform the government about any problems or grievances within the nation

🪙 Ministry of Treasury 🪙

Emir of Treasury - FirePlacki
The creation, management and spending of Wealth within the nation.
Grinding for resources such as Netherite and XP
Grinding any material the nation requires (build materials)
Produce gear, brews and other supplies needed for combat
Run internal and external markets, business and trade

🎭 Ministry of Culture 🎭

Emir of Culture - Kami Lah-Sitara
Develops the national culture in RP, Lore and OOC, ingame and over Discord.
Write lore, promote roleplay and worldbuild for the nation
Prepare events and festivals for the nation
Produce media and memes for the nation
Improve the life quality within the nation

🛠️ Ministry of Infrastructure 🛠️

Emir of Infrastructure - MasonMFS
Build big national build projects (castles, churchers, monuments, etc.)
Build residential houses, shops and other urban development
Build roads, bridges and other transportation infrastructure
Maintain nations wilderness
Design builds/schematics on nations build server
Build economic structures (animal farms, cactus XP farms, etc.)
Build defensive structures (walls, trenches, traps, etc.)
Tak on build job postings
Organizes and builds economic structures, such as for example; cactus XP farms
Posts and takes on build job postings/organizes and manages build job posting channel
