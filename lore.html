Histography of the the Vacan people

Many years ago, in the vast expanse of the desert landscape, there used to be several small settlements, with various names and all kinds of cultures, trying their best to survive and secure a lasting existence through their descendants. However, as the population within these settlements kept increasing, it turned out to be quite a challenge. With their immense growth, their vast supply of provisions soon began to dwindle by the day. Water had already been hard to obtain before, but it got even worse with the continuously thriving population. As food became scarce, the settlements began to fight over the shares that were left in their scorching desert. Some tried to flee from the sandy prison they called home, but to no avail… before even getting close to its borders, their ever decreasing rations were depleted, leading to their inevitable demise. An all out war ensued shortly after, chaos, bloodshed and fear. The once golden desert tainted with the outcome of the inhumane brutality caused by their impending doom.
